The leader in online test prep

TESTfunda.com is a for-profit education website based in Mumbai, India. In 2008, four IIT and IIM alumni founded the website.[1] The website provides help to students, ranging from career counseling to preparation. It provides assistance for MBA exam preparation for CAT, XAT, SNAP, IIFT, NMAT, CMAT for admission to IIMs and other MBA institutes.
